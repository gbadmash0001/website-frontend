Tournament Rules

1. Don’t use hacks, panels, or any cheating tools. If you're caught, you'll be removed and may lose your wallet money.

2. Leaving during a match or being inactive will get you disqualified.

3. If your internet or device fails, matches won’t be restarted. Please check your setup before joining.

4. If something goes wrong during a match, show proof like a screenshot or video. The admin will review everything before deciding.

5. Don’t destroy the other player's gloo wall. If it happens by mistake, the admin may forgive you. But if you do it on purpose, the reward will go to the other player—even if you win.

6. The admin will record every match you play.

7. If you want headshot-only mode, let the admin know when they contact you.

8. Grenades are not allowed in any match type.

9. You can jump over an obstacle and enter the opponent's gloo wall to eliminate them—this move is allowed.
